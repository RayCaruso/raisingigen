Parenting with Purpose

Survival of the Focused

Parenting with Purpose provides the blueprint that enables us to shift from parenting to survive, to raising the next generation to thrive - in a distracted world. It’s survival of the focused. This canvass provides the necessary map for modeling, teaching, and focusing wellbeing and purpose for ourselves, our families, and our children.

As Socrates taught “the unexamined life is not worth living.” The first step educates and solidifies a strong sense of what it is to be “me,” providing the foundations for examining well-being, mindfulness and happiness. The next step demonstrates why we must evolve our thinking into a “survival of the focused” mentality. The tipping point is narrowly defining how the “me” fits into the “we” allowing us to define and illustrate our purpose and joy.
